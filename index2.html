About Us


About Dragon Pool

Dragon Pool is a game that completely combines the NFT IO-game platform and the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), the achievements in the game will be recorded on the Blockchain and basically determine the NFT denomination.
Each player owns a collection of one or more dragons using the NFT protocol. Players will use their Dragons to engage in battles with thousands of other opponents, with the goal of collecting and fighting.

Legend has it that the dragon that survives the last in the battle will become the Divine Dragon, uniting the 5 tribes to rule and become invincible.

GamePlay

At Dragon Pool, all you have to do is eat, survive, fight.

You will have to control your Dragon to fly in the PlayZone, eat tokens to grow in size and level, dodge or destroy other opponents.




Evolution

Earn Mechanism

Trading

You can invest dragon eggs to try your luck with rare dragons. Or own and raise dragons to higher levels, sell dragons and items on the Marketplace.

Playing

Join the battle, with your skills to collect or win other opponents to own DP-NFT Token.

Reward

Participate in monthly events, perform daily tasks, fight for position in the gold table, you will receive appropriate rewards.

Referral

Share this game to your friends, you will receive a commission corresponding to 1% of the value they earn.
